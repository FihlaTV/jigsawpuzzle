About this project

This is a jigsaw puzzle webapp in html5 jigsaw made with Scirra Construct2 (capx will be posted soon).

Main features (as of v1.4.0.5):

  • You can select between two tilesets (48 pieces or 108 pieces).
  • There are three pictures you can choose from (more pictures will be added in the future).
  • You can select whether the pieces have black borders or not.
  • You can switch between two different background colors or show the original image at background as hint.
  • Game is timed.
